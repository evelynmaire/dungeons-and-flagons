About

Something unique and powerful

Dungeons & Flagons is the brainchild of local entrepreneurs Ryuu Shibutani and Sonja Arneson.

The world of role-playing games and fantasy had always been a huge part of our lives. Playing Dungeons and Dragons with our friends through high school and rushing to Friday Night Magic sessions after school.

While our parents were happy to let us cram our late-night RPG sessions into each others basements or tiny kicthens, we came to relaise not everybody had the luxury of like-minded friends and guardians, so in 2014 we quit our day-jobs in HR and International Business to open a restaurant.

Since then, Dungeons & Flagons has gone from strength to strength, providing weekly entertainment to the residents of San Fransisco and providing great food and live music.
